Project Objective

Atliq Hardwares, a fictional company, is a major computer hardware maker in India and abroad. Yet, they lack quick insights for smart decisions. So, an SQL challenge is on the horizon to fill this data gap.

Our mission in the Sales Performance Enhancement project was to empower decision-makers with actionable insights. Focused on boosting sales, key priorities included:

  • Informing Decision-Making Strategies
  • Enhancing Overall Sales Performance

Skills & Techniques Used

  • Proficiency in SQL data querying for business insights
  • Strategic decision-making with data-driven approaches
  • Selection of effective visualizations for reporting
  • Implementation of SQL solutions in a business context
  • Comprehensive analysis of key sales metrics

This project helps AtliQ Hardwares make faster and smarter business decisions using SQL-driven insights. By analyzing key sales data, the company can optimize performance, improve efficiency, and drive higher revenue.
