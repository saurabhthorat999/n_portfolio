Seasonal Sales Trends

Sales peak in Q3 and Q4 (October–March) due to major Indian festivals such as Diwali and New Year.

Q1 and Q2 (April–September) generally witness slower sales, making it necessary for companies to implement alternative marketing strategies.

Manufacturers should optimize their sales and marketing strategies to align with these trends for maximum impact.
